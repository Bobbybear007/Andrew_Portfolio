Key Game Jams

The Beginners Jam Summer 2024

The Special Time Fixing Unit

The Special Time Fixing Unit is a sci-fi puzzle-platformer where players take on the role of a trainee in a future where time travel is possible—but with high stakes. The slightest error in time manipulation could result in catastrophic consequences, endangering both the world and the fabric of space-time. As a recruit in the Special Time Fixing Unit, your goal is to prevent these disasters by mastering time travel and working with your past selves to solve complex puzzles and avoid paradoxes. The game was created for the Beginner’s Jam Summer 2024, aligning with the theme “Outside the Boundaries.” Time travel is a key mechanic that challenges players to step beyond the typical boundaries of time to progress. With retro-styled 2D art and a thoughtful combination of puzzle-solving and platforming, the game provides a rewarding experience for those who enjoy mind-bending challenges

Game Dev Palmy Game Jam

Twisted Depths

Twisted Depths is an adventure-platformer game where players explore a mysterious mineshaft beneath their house, only to uncover something strange and unexpected. The game was developed as part of the Palmy Game Jam and features a side-scrolling format. The environment is designed with 3D art by Andrew Zambazos, while Beetruth handled the coding and technical aspects. The game is available to play on multiple platforms, including Windows, macOS, and HTML5.
